Executive Proposal Summary

Sirius MTP serves as Zambia's digital mining infrastructure platform supporting local content, ASM formalization, supplier development, critical minerals investment, finance access and mineral trade transparency.

Strategic Benefits

  • Formalize ASM mining and trade.
  • Increase local supplier participation.
  • Connect miners to financiers.
  • Support MMMD reporting and compliance.
  • Promote investment into critical minerals.
